Download Some Games

You already have points!

You'll automatically get a few games with your account. When you're interested in trying more games, you can access the Sifteo Store by clicking on the "Sifteo Store" tab.

Some games are free, and others are paid for with points. You can see your points balance in the top right-hand corner of Siftrunner. If you use up all your points, you can buy more in the Sifteo store.
